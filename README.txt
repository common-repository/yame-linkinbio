Generates an Instagram page where you can showcase your Instagram posts with a custom link and text.

== Description ==
If you are looking for an easy way to gain more leads and clicks from your instagram followers then Yame\'s Link In Bio plugin is something for you.

Create a clickable tiled Instagram linked page with your best posts. Tag them with a custom label and link them to a unique url.

If you have feature requests, issues or founnd a bug. Message me yannick@yame.be.

== Installation ==

How to get started:

1) Go to settings > LinkInBio
2) Provide your Instagram username
3) Insert a custom bio
4) Click on images to add them to your page
5) Provide text and links for each of your image

Now you can use the [linkinbio] shortcode.
